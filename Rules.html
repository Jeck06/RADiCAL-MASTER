About RADiCAL MASTER

RADiCAL MASTER is an innovative digital-based learning module-application (Mod-App) Proposed by the researchers of the study entitled "Improving the Math Fluency of Grade 9 Students: Basis for Digital-based Instruction Materials". Its goal is to help enhance the math fluency of Students on Radicals. Designed to provide an interactive and immersive learning experience for learners focusing on the concept of Radicals in mathematics. With a range of features and resources, RADiCAL MASTERY aims to enhance learners' understanding and mastery of Radicals through conceptual explanations, visual representations, practice exercises, real-world examples, gamification elements, additional resources, and collaboration features.
