Jerick D. Aleniado

Jerick D. Aleniado is a talented and passionate individual who has dedicated himself to the field of education and mathematics. At the age of 22, he is a third-year BSEd Math major student at Tiwi Community College, where he has been actively pursuing his passion for teaching and learning.

Jerick's academic journey has been marked by his involvement as one of the researchers in a groundbreaking study entitled "Improving the Math Fluency of Grade 9 Students: Basis for Digital-based Instruction Materials." This research project aimed to develop digital-based instructional materials that would enhance the math fluency of grade 9 students, with a particular focus on the concept of Radicals.

During the course of this study, Jerick's expertise and dedication shone through, and he played a pivotal role in the development of RADiCAL MASTER, an innovative digital-based learning module-application (Mod-App). As one of the developers, Jerick contributed his knowledge, skills, and creativity to design a platform that provides an interactive and immersive learning experience for learners.

RADiCAL MASTER is a culmination of the researchers' vision to enhance learners' understanding and mastery of Radicals in mathematics. It incorporates a wide range of features and resources, including conceptual explanations, visual representations, practice exercises, real-world examples, gamification elements, additional resources, and collaboration features. Jerick's contribution to this project has been instrumental in creating a platform that aims to improve math fluency and make learning Radicals an engaging and effective experience for students.

With his dedication to education and his commitment to improving math fluency, Jerick is making a significant impact on the field of mathematics education. Through his involvement in the development of RADiCAL MASTER, he has demonstrated his ability to merge innovative technology with pedagogical principles, resulting in a valuable resource for learners.

As Jerick continues his academic journey and pursues his career in education, he remains focused on empowering students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in mathematics. His passion, hard work, and dedication serve as an inspiration to others and highlight his potential to contribute further to the field of education.
